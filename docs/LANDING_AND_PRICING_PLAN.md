# Landing Page & Pricing Page – Improvement Plan

This doc is a plan to move the landing and pricing pages from prototype feel to launch-ready: clearer copy, better UX, and placeholders that look credible until you have real reviews and analytics.

---

## 1. What’s already done

- **Pricing:** Home link added to top-right of nav (with Pricing, Dashboard).
- **Landing:** Stats updated to early-stage style (suggestions delivered, avg. response time, calls coached, “Early access”).
- **Landing:** Testimonials replaced with realistic placeholder quotes (SDR, Founder, AE; specific use cases, no fake “40% more deals” claims).
- **Landing (full build-out):** Hero subcopy tightened + “Who it’s for” line; product overview headline/copy; value prop “Real-time coaching, not post-call notes”; how it works steps with concrete lines (Enter, buttons, notes, save transcript); features grid reduced to 4 cards matching product (Live transcript, What to say / Questions / Key points, Notes + AI rewrite, Save transcript); final CTA “Start your free trial” + Compare plans; “See it in action” link under hero.
- **Navbar:** “Start free trial” button added in nav (desktop); mobile already had Download.
- **Footer:** “Start free trial” label on CTA; Pricing link fixed to `/pricing`.
- **Pricing page:** Hero subcopy “Start free. Upgrade when you’re ready.”; plan features aligned with product (Starter/Pro bullets); FAQ “How does the free trial work?” added; CTA section “Start your free trial” + “Back to home” secondary.

---

## 2. Landing page – copy & structure

| Area | Current | Proposed |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Hero** | “Sales calls are hard. Persuaid guides you on what to say.” | Keep headline; tighten subcopy to one clear promise (e.g. “AI listens to your call and suggests what to say next—and questions to ask—in real time.”). |
| **Product overview** | “Your control center for winning conversations” | Align with product: “See your live transcript and AI suggestions in one place. Press Enter for what to say next.” |
| **Value prop** | “Why sales teams choose Persuaid” | Option: “Built for reps who want a coach in the call” or “Real-time coaching, not post-call notes.” |
| **How it works** | 3 steps (Connect → Guidance → Close) | Keep 3 steps; add one concrete line each (e.g. “Press Enter for a suggested line and questions.”). |
| **Features grid** | 6 generic feature blurbs | Match actual product: Live transcript, What to say / Questions / Key points, Notes + AI rewrite, Save transcript. |
| **Testimonials** | Placeholder quotes (done) | When you have real users, swap in real quotes and add “Beta user” or “Early access user” if needed. |
| **Stats** | Product/early metrics (done) | When you have real data, replace with: calls coached, suggestions used, or NPS/satisfaction if you survey. |
| **Final CTA** | “Ready to close more deals?” | Keep or narrow: “Start your free trial” + one secondary (See how it works / Pricing). |

**UX tweaks**

- Add a short “Who it’s for” line under the hero (e.g. “For SDRs, AEs, and founders who run their own demos.”).
- Ensure primary CTA is always “Download” or “Start free trial” and goes to `/download` (or signup).
- Optional: one “See it in action” link that scrolls to product preview or a 30s Loom later.

## 4. Shared (Navbar & Footer)

- **Nav:** Home is top-right (done). Consider adding “Download” or “Start free trial” as a prominent button in the nav on both pages.

## 5. When you have real data

1. **Testimonials:** Replace placeholder quotes with real ones; add name, role, company (with permission), and optionally “Beta user” or “Early customer.”
2. **Stats:** Replace “10k+ suggestions”, “~2s”, “500+ calls”, “Early access” with real metrics (e.g. “X calls coached”, “Y suggestions delivered”, “Z teams in early access”).
3. **Social proof:** Add logos (with permission) or “Used by teams at [industries]” when you have a few customers.
4. **Pricing:** If you run promos (e.g. founding customer discount), add a small badge or line under the hero.

---

## 6. Suggested next steps (priority)

1. **Copy pass:** Apply the hero and product-overview line changes on the landing page (Section 2 table).
2. **Features grid:** Update the 6 feature cards to match the actual app (transcript, follow-up buttons, notes, save transcript).
3. **Pricing features:** Align plan bullets with real features (Starter vs Pro vs Enterprise).
4. **FAQ:** Add the free-trial question on the pricing page.
5. **Nav CTA:** Add a clear “Download” or “Start free trial” button in the navbar (in addition to the scroll-triggered one).

You can tick these off as you implement them and replace placeholders with real testimonials and stats when ready.
